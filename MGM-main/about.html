About Our School

Nurturing Excellence Since 2000

MGM Public School

MGM Public School is a sister Concern of MGEMHSS, founded in the year 2000. Dedicated to the sacred memory of Mar Gregorious of Parumala, we maintain high academic standards affiliated with CBSE, New Delhi.

The school intends to impart quality education, so as to bring out best citizens for Mother India. We give top priority for character formation of pupils. Imparting quality education for a better tomorrow is our motto.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in education, fostering intellectual growth, ethical values, and social responsibility in every student to face the global challenges.

Our Mission

Establishing high-quality educational standards, focusing on character building, spiritual awakening, and the holistic development of our students through modern pedagogy.

Principal's Message

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world. At MGM, we focus not just on academic scores, but on the integrity and character of our students."

Mrs. Sumany P Joy
